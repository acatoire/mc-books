This website provides translations for various Minecraft add-ons, initially generated by AI and then reviewed by human.

Naturalist Add-On

Currently, we are focusing on translating the Naturalist Add-On, which adds a variety of new animals and creatures to your Minecraft world. Our translations include detailed information about each creature, their behaviors, and interactions.
